Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect the personal information of users who interact with our data analysis tutoring application. We are committed to safeguarding user privacy and ensuring the confidentiality of any data collected.

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: We may collect personal information such as names, email addresses, and contact details when users register for the platform.
  • Usage Data: Information about how users interact with the platform, including pages visited, assessments taken, and progress through the learning journey.
  • Assessment Data: Responses to initial and progress assessments used to create personalized learning journeys.

3. How We Use Information

  • Personalization: To create tailored learning experiences based on user assessments.
  • Platform Improvement: To analyze usage data and improve the quality and functionality of the platform.
  • Communication: To send updates, respond to inquiries, and provide information related to the platform and user progress.
  • Certification: To generate certificates with users' full names upon completion of courses.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share personal information with third parties except as required by law or to provide the services requested by users (e.g., certificate generation).

Third-party service providers that assist in the operation of the platform may have access to personal information, but only to the extent necessary to perform their functions.

5. Data Security

We take data security seriously and implement appropriate measures to protect user data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. This includes secure servers, encryption, and access controls.

6. User Rights

  • Access: Users have the right to access the personal information we hold about them.
  • Correction: Users may request corrections to their personal information if it is inaccurate or incomplete.
  • Deletion: Users may request the deletion of their personal information, subject to certain limitations (e.g., legal obligations).

7. Cookies and Tracking

Our platform may use cookies to enhance user experience and gather information about platform usage. Users can control cookie preferences through their browser settings.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Users will be notified of significant changes through the platform or by email.
