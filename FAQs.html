Frequently Asked Questions

What is DataTutor?
DataTutor is an online platform designed to help users master data analysis through personalized learning experiences. We provide courses in tools such as Excel, MS SQL, Power BI, and Python.
How do I register for DataTutor?
To register, simply visit our registration page, fill in your details, and click the "Register" button. You will receive a confirmation email once your registration is complete.
What courses are offered?
We offer a range of courses including Excel, MS SQL, Power BI, and Python. These courses are designed to take you from beginner to advanced proficiency, with hands-on projects and personalized learning paths.
Is there a certification after completing the courses?
Yes, you will receive a certificate upon successfully completing each course. The certificates are provided in PDF format and can be downloaded from your profile.
How much does it cost to join DataTutor?
We offer multiple pricing plans depending on the course or learning path you choose. Please visit our pricing page for more details on current plans and offers.
Can I access DataTutor from my mobile device?
Yes, our platform is mobile-friendly and can be accessed from any device, including smartphones and tablets.
